This repository contains a collection of Explainer Pages — simple, transparent HTML documents that explain the identity, purpose, and relationships of organizations, brands, and projects.

These pages are designed to provide clear factual explanations separate from marketing websites. Each page serves as a long-term reference document linking to the official website of the organization described.
